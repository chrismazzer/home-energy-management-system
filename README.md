# Home Energy Management System

## Overview

The Home Energy Management System (HEMS) is a modular embedded platform designed to monitor, control and optimise residential energy systems.

The long-term goal is to design a scalable system capable of monitoring batteries, solar generation and household loads while communicating over CAN bus and integrating with Home Assistant.

## Objectives

- Battery monitoring
- Battery management
- CAN bus communication
- Energy monitoring
- Power electronics
- MPPT solar charging
- Embedded firmware
- PCB design
- Mechanical design
- Home Assistant integration
